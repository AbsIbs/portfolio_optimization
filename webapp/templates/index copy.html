BUSINESS PROBLEM

For decades, countless studies have been conducted to predict the stock market and maximise returns. Efficient Market Hypothesis and Random Walk Hypothesis argue that this is a pointless endeavor as stock prices follow a random order.

My project proposes to shift attention from a predictive focus to a reactive one. One where machine learning is used to determine what action to take based on previous situations. Reinforcement Learning will be done to achieve this.

Ultimately, my project aims to solve the problem of, ‘given what happened in the market yesterday, what is the best way to allocate my portfolio of assets?’.
